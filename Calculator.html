What Are the Functions on the Calculator?

This is a simple calculator with four basic arithmetic operations. Here’s what each symbol means:

  • + Addition – Adds two numbers (e.g., 5 + 2 = 7)
  • − Subtraction – Subtracts one number from another (e.g., 9 − 4 = 5)
  • × Multiplication – Multiplies two numbers (e.g., 3 × 4 = 12)
  • ÷ Division – Divides one number by another (e.g., 8 ÷ 2 = 4)

You can use the number buttons and operators to enter expressions. Click = to calculate. Press AC to clear the screen. You can also use your keyboard!
